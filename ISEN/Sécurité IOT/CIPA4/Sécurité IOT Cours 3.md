## Radio Frequencies & Wireless Protocols
- **Frequencies**: 2.4 Ghz band, **2412 MHz - 2462 MHz**
- **Standard Protocols**: Compliant with **IEEE 802.11b/g** and **802.11n** (20M/40M) standards
- **Modulation**:
	- **802.11b**: DSSS
	- **802.11g/n**: OFDM
- **Security/Encryption**: Supports **WEP**, **WPA**, and **WPA2** encryption.   
- **Antenna**: Uses an integral antenna with a gain of **0dBi**

## Base Network & Video Protocols
- **Network Protocols**: Supports HTTP, TCP/IP, UDP, SMTP, DDNS, SNTP, DHCP, and FTP   
- **Video Compression**: Uses **MJPEG** hardware compression technology for video transmission
